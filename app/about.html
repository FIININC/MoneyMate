About Us

For the majority of us in society, living paycheque-to-paycheque is an unfortunate reality. Most of the times we are able to get by, but sometimes we find ourselves at odds with being able to pay for a very basic need that is essential to our lives (such as rent, groceries, and electricity bills), or to cover for an unexpected emergency (like medical costs or a car breakdown). At these troubling times, it seems like nobody is there to lend us a hand and the banks won’t approve us for a personal loan. We then find ourselves rushing for a cash advance loan, and taking out a payday loan becomes inevitable. There seems to be no real solution to our problems, and the only one that exists seems to take advantage of our circumstances and make matters worse for us.

MoneyMate is a first of its kind online cash loan service that builds a savings deposit for the borrower. It gives consumers fast and easy access to a short-term loan in times of desperate need, while simultaneously helping them create a savings buffer so they can recover from their financial struggles, so that they can avoid being short on cash for any upcoming bills or unforeseen expenses.

Our mission is to END the debt-trap cycle that affects millions of people around the world because of the predatory practices they are subject to from the use of payday loans. Users of these loans are financially vulnerable consumers that lack access to healthy empowering loans and MoneyMate is on a quest to change that!
