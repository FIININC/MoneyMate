Disclosures

Payday Loans are high cost loans. Loan availability and loan amounts vary by location. To qualify for a payday loan, minimum net pay requirements and other conditions will apply. Lending decisions and funding times are subject to system limitations.

MoneyMate® Payday Loans are offered only to residents of Ontario, Canada. The cost of borrowing is $15.00 per $100.00 for each $100.00 advanced. On a $300.00 loan for 60 days, the cost of borrowing is $45.00, with a total payback amount of $345.00. The maximum allowable cost of borrowing under the Payday Loan Act, 2008 is $15.00 per each $100.00 advanced.
