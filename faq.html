FAQs

What is the Savings and how does it work ?

The Savings is a feature that is incorporated into the loan. We essentially set aside a portion of your payments towards your Savings and then direct deposit it into your account once you’ve payed off your loan.

What do I need to be eligible for this loan ?

The requirements to apply and have a good shot in getting approved for our cash advance loans are:

  • Be 18 years of age or older and provide us with valid proof of your government ID
  • Working telephone number and a valid email address•Provide proof of a recurring source of income
  • Have a current bank account in Canada
What if I have bad credit or no credit history, can I still get approved for your loans ?

Absolutely! Our service is geared to those who have bad credit and have a hard time getting approved for a personal loan. We don’t do a credit check and we don’t base our decisions on your credit report, we use other criteria to evaluate you as a borrower.

When can I expect to receive my cash advance ?

Even though we disburse your funds immediately after approval, the money usually takes some time to arrive in your bank account. If it was up to us you would have the cash in your account right away, but unfortunately we still have to wait on the banks to process the payments so that you get the money in your bank account. Depending on the bank you deal with and the time of day you apply with us, this can take anywhere from 1 hour to 24 hours hours.

What if I just want a cash advance loan without the Savings part ?

Unfortunately this is not an option. The Savings is part of our loan offering. We do this to help you better budget for the foreseeable future so that you are better off financially after you’ve payed off the loan, and with that help improve your economic well-being.

I got approved for your cash loan, what do I do next ?

After filling out the application form and getting approved, sit back and let us handle the rest. We will disburse your cash advance immediately and then periodically withdraw your payments from your bank account as agreed upon in your contract. Make sure to have sufficient funds in your bank account when payments are due in order to avoid extra costs. At the end we will make a final payment to you in the form of your Savings.

When can I expect to receive my Savings ?

After you’ve payed off your online loan in full, we will deposit the Savings you have been accumulating during your time with us directly into your bank account. You will receive your Savings within a week of the final payment you made towards your cash loan.

How are you cheaper than payday loans ?

This requires a little bit of math to explain but don’t worry its very simple.

Suppose you take an online payday loan for $300. The fee you will pay on that will be $45 ($15 per every $100 of cash advance). Now because you have to pay back the payday loan + fee in 2 weeks, the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) on that loan will be:

[ fee / loan ] x [ total days in a year / term of the loan ] x 100 = APR
[ $45 / $300 ] x [ 365 days / 14 days ] x 100% = 391%

Using the same variables for a MoneyMate loan, but with a 2 month repayment period instead, we get:

[ $45 / $300 ] x [ 365 days / 60 days ] x 100% = 91%
