How It Works

1

Select Your Loan Preferences

Select a loan amount based on how much of a cash advance you need to take care of your most important and immediate bills. Next, select the savings % you would like to accumulate during the repayment of the loan (this percentage is based on the cash amount you chose and will be added to your total repayment). Last, select your recurring income periodical. Click APPLY NOW.

Fill Out The Application Form

Fill out the Application Form with your personal information. You will need two pieces of government identification. Fill out your employment and income information. You will need to provide us a copy of your regular recurring income. Fill out your banking information. Make sure that this account is the one that contains your paycheque direct deposits. Upload your personal, income, and banking documentation with all the relevant information and click Submit.

Receive Your Funds

Once you submit the application, sit back and relax, let us take care of the rest. We will process your application - and if approved - we will release your funds immediately. You will receive the cash advance in your bank account within a maximum of 24 hours.

Receive Your Savings!

We will make timely withdrawals for the repayment of your cash advance loan based on your income payment periods. Make sure to have sucient money in the bank to fulll your loan obligations and receive your Savings. Once you’ve payed o the loan, we will direct deposit the Savings you’ve accumulated into your bank account within a week. You can use the Savings in case you run short on cash again, or for your simple enjoyment :) Don’t forget to let us know what you will do with your Savings!
